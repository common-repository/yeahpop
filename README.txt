Display Your Recent WooCommerce Sales To Increase Your Conversion Rate.

== Description ==

Use social proof to get more sales by informing your visitors when and what products have been recently bought in your WooCommerce store.
YeahPop for WooCommerce doesn't require you to sign up for third party services.
Add small, non-intrusive popup notifications to your site in seconds.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the `yeahpop` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in wordpress
3. Done!

== Screenshots ==

1. YeahPop shows your recent orders as a clean non-intrusive pop up.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How many orders will Yeahpop show =

YeahPop takes the last 5 orders for a product and loops them as notifications.
Each notification lasts 8 seconds and appears within 8 seconds intervals.

= What information is shown? =

Notifications show the buyer's name, location and how long ago they purchased.

= Is YeahPop responsive? =

Yes, notifications will appear on all devices.
